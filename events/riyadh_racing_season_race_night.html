Riyadh Racing Season Race Night

Unlimited Action!

Starting from: 13 March 2025 - 13 March 2025

Location: King Abdulaziz Equestrian Field, Riyadh

About the event

A race night at King Abdulaziz Racecourse is an evening like no other. The jewel in the crown of Saudi racing, this sophisticated venue features picturesque viewing lawns, stylish lounges and restaurants and, of course, proximity to the thrilling racing action. Tickets and award-winning all-inclusive hospitality experiences are available (tickets for under 16s are free when accompanied by an adult). The racecourse has free car parking. Gates open at 8pm. First race 9pm and last race is at 12.30pm.
