Arab Music Concert

A night of Folclore

Starting from: 31 March 2025

Location: Dammam rcuit

About the event

A Concert in Dammam for Arab Music lovers and artists. This event will be held on 31 March 2025 at Dammam rcuit. It will feature a diverse lineup of talented musicians and singers, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Arab music. The event will begin at 6:00 PM and will conclude at 10:00 PM. Tickets are available online and at the door. Don't miss out on this unforgettable night of music and culture!
