English Club

A Culture Event for English Learners

Starting from: 03 May 2025 - 09 May 2025

Location: Khafji rcuit

About the event

The English Club is a unique cultural event designed for English learners of all levels. Join us for a week of immersive language experiences, engaging workshops, and interactive activities that will enhance your English skills while exploring the rich culture of English-speaking countries. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced learner, this event offers something for everyone. Don't miss this opportunity to connect with fellow language enthusiasts and improve your English in a fun and supportive environment.
